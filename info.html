Information About Safety

Azerbaijan is generally considered a safe destination for tourists, with low crime rates in most areas, including major cities like Baku. The local authorities prioritize the safety and security of visitors, and tourist areas are well-monitored. Travelers are advised to exercise standard precautions, such as safeguarding personal belongings and avoiding isolated areas at night. Road conditions in urban centers are good, though caution is recommended when traveling in rural areas. Emergency services are accessible by dialing 102 for police, 103 for medical assistance, and 101 for fire emergencies.

Visit the official Azerbaijan Travel website for more information.

Travel Tip

Carry some cash as not all places accept cards, especially in rural areas.
